It's summertime and Red, the bird, is hungry. He went to The Heavenly Peach Garden, to have some juicy peaches. But sadly for him, the pig is also there who won't let him eat peacefully. The pig challenged Red for a competition of eating peaches.


The number on a cell represents the number of peaches on the tree in its place. The bird and the pig make moves alternatively with Red starting first. In each move, the player can go to its adjacent cell and eat all the peaches on that tree.

Help Red eat more peaches than the pig.
